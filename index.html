How To Play
  • To start place your mouse in the gray box.
  • When you move your mouse off of the box, it will change color. Move to the opposite box of the same color and repeat.
  • Hitting the correct color will give 200 points, but the wrong color will take away 100.
  • However, now and then you will get a liar, with a black outline.
  • Liars give 500 point if you choose a different color and take 200 away if you chose the 'correct' color.
  • You will loose points over time, and the higher level (round) you are the faster you loose points.
  • See how long you can keep your points in the positive!
Good luck!
