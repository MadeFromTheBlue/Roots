To start place your mouse in the gray box.

When you move your mouse off of the gray box, it will change color. Move to the opposite box of the same color and repeat.

Hitting the correct color will give 200 points, but the wrong color will take away 100.

You will loose points over time, and the higher level you are the faster you loose points.

You will stop loosing points when your mouse is in a grey box.

See how long you can keep your points in the positive.
